Amber Wireless Pulse meter adapter AMB8568-M

Amber wireless is a very simple yet dependable transmitter operating in the M-bus Wireless standard, which can be connected to any meter with a pulse output (S0 output). This is typically an electric, gas or water meter. The transmitter converts the pulse output to a wireless radio transmission, frequency 868MHz, which is then further processed in the concentrator.

  • Wireless M-Bus adapter for meters with pulse interface
  • Over-the-air (OTA) setup (e.g. pulse valence, initial meter value)
  • Internal 3.6 V battery, achievable battery life > 12 years
  • Low energy: Power saving modes, fast wake-up times
  • Configurable data records, prepared for utility meters
  • Encryption using AES128 (Mode 5) + CMAC (Mode 7)
  • Pulse input supports both open collector and potential-free pulse outputs
  • 4 pulse inputs
  • Optional tamper detection
  • Operating modes S, T and C mode according to EN 13757-4
  • Radiated power: Typ. 8 dBm (transmitter class HT)
  • Frequency range: 868.3 or 868.95 MHz
  • Modulation 2-FSK
  • Dimensions: 80 x 120 x 57 mm without cable gland/101 x 120 x 57 mm with cable gland
