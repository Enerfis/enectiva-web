Compact Ultrasonic Meter Sharky 774

The design and design of the SHARKY 774 subcompact ultrasonic heat meter is based on the proven and popular SHARKY 775 meter. The meter is designed for installation in return pipes and hot water heating systems. The calorimetric counter is removable from the flowmeter.
