Electrometer PRO380-Mb 0,25-100A M-Bus

In Enectiva we use electrometers from PRO380 series with a range of 4 modules for three phase electricity measuring, each type with specific characteristics. They all have a high accuracy class. The PRO380 series is available as a standard version (with a S0 output) and also as a Modbus or M-bus version.


    Three-phase, single-dual-rate meter on the DIN rail
  • direct measurement up to 100A
  • officially certified meter by MID (Measuring Instrument Directive)
  • Accuracy class 1
  • DIN-rail mounting, only 4 modules
  • 2x pulse output SO + LED 0.1- 10,000 imp. /kWh (programmable)
  • 1x SO pulse output for: consumption

  • 1x SO pulse output for: supply
  • LCD display 6 + 2
  • measurement subscription-supply
  • M-Bus interface (standard)
  • infrared interface (standard)
  • Dimension: 141 x 70 x 63 mm
  • Weight 500 g
