ULTRAHEAT T550 Ultrasonic Metering

ULTRAHEAT T550 Ultrasonic Metering Is designed for a variety of heat / cold measurements. The modularity and flexibility of individual software settings make it ideal to adapt to customer requirements and usage.



ULTRAHEAT T550 Ultrasonic Metering

Functions and features
  • High precision and measurement stability thanks to the ultrasonic measurement principle
  • Wear-resistant, as it is free of moving parts
  • It normally includes a log of traffic
  • An approved 1: 100 measurement range
  • No reassuring lengths are required before and behind the meter
  • All-metal measuring tube
  • Battery life up to 16 years
  • Power supplies from 24V AC / DC to 230V
  • Optical interface according to EN 62056-21: 2002
  • Two slots for communication modules
  • Allows you to read data for the last 60 months
  • The number of tariff functions allows customization of the device to individual requirements
  • Rugged, robust, wear-resistant
  • Self-diagnosis and automatic fault detection
  • An optional programmable Datalogger for system monitoring
Communication interface
  • Impulse module, 2 channels
  • M-bus module
  • M-Bus with two pulse inputs
  • Current loop module
  • Analog module, 2 channels
  • Radio module with two pulse inputs
  • GPRS / SMS module with two pulse inputs
  • GPRS module for connecting 8 M-Bus meters
