Integral-MK MaXX Compact Heat Meter

The Compact Heat Meter with Capsule Flow Meter, EAT Assembly Kit, and Rotary Valve Unit have electronic V-Speed Speed Detection. The design is in accuracy class 2 according to ČSN EN 1434, validation period is 4 years. It can not be affected by a magnet, it is easily repairable. It is equipped with an ASIC circuit with high integration.
