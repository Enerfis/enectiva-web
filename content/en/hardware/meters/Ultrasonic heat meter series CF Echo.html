Ultrasonic heat meter series CF Echo

Compact ultrasonic heat meter with integrated parts, flowmeter with evaluation unit and temperature smelter Pt 100 (Pt 500) has a modular construction of accuracy class 2 according to ČSN EN 1434, validation period 4 years, environment class A, PTB class C. The advantage is easy Installation, minimal maintenance and long service life. Suitable for heating applications, combined heating and cooling. It has high flow dynamics.
