WFN and WFR volumetric heating and cooling meters (MEGATRON 2 – SIEMECA).

Electronic heat and cold meters WFN and WFR (MEGATRON 2 - SIEMECA) are used for measuring heat and cooling energy. Their main area of application is in central heating and cooling facilities where heat and cooling energy is delivered individually to multiple users. Typical users are private property owners, real estate companies, asset management companies, real estate agencies, and more.



WFN and WFR volumetric heating and cooling meters

Function:
  • Measurement of heat and cold consumption from flow and temperature difference
  • Accumulation of consumption values
  • Saving consumption values to the specified day
  • Display consumption values
  • View important traffic data
  • Self-diagnosis with fault indication
  • Data transfer via M-Bus or wireless at 868 MHz

Display
  • Cumulated heat and cold consumption
  • Display segment test
  • Instantaneous heat and cold consumption
  • Instantaneous flow
  • Instantaneous inlet water temperature
  • Instantaneous return water temperature
  • Instantaneous temperature difference
  • Battery operating hours
  • Day of deduction
  • Saved heat and cold consumption over the past 13 months
  • check code
  • Fault indication

Fault messages
  • Temporary disturbances (do not affect the correct measurement function)
  • Serious malfunctions (measurement stopped, display alternately shows the fault code and the date when the fault first occurred
Specifications
  • Measurement accuracy class 3 according to EN 1434
  • Environmental class A according to EN 1434
  • Energy units kWh (GJ)
  • Max. Measurable heat output 300 kW
  • Flow rates 0.6 m3 / h; 1.5 m3 / h; 2.5 m3 / h
  • Heating water quality according to VDI 2035
  • Max. Operating pressure 16 bar
  • Temperature range of flowmeter 1 ... 90 ° C
  • Measuring heating temperatures of 20 ... 110 ° C
  • Measuring cooling temperatures of 1 ... 16 ° C
  • Output signal when selecting M-Bus
  • Output signal when testing optionally M-Bus, voltage pulses
  • Degree of protection IP54
  • Battery life> 8 years (> 7 years with radio)
  • Connection cable of temperature sensor 1,5 or 2,5 m

M-Bus connection kit
  • WFZ.MBUSSET, cable with connector, length 1 m
