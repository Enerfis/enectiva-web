Integral-V MaXX Compact Heat Meter

Compact electronic heat meter with ČSN EN 1434 accuracy class. Tamper proof and equipped with high density ASIC circuit.


Electronic compact heat meter Integral-VMaXX, TH series IV

Technical specifications:
  • Temperature range: 0 – 90 °C(standard) / 0 - 140°C (optional)
  • Temperature difference dT: 3-70°C (standard) / 3-120°C (optional)
  • Enthalpy correction: automatic
  • EPROM: Yes
  • Automatic reading of end-of-month consumption
  • Mounting: vertical and horizontal
  • Temperature sensor (Tmax 90 ° C), silicone cable (Tmax 130 ° C), direct draft (Tmax 140 ° C)
  • Power: 3,6 V Lithium Battery Battery life: 10 years
  • External communication:
  • - selectable communication cards
  • -M BUS / 4 external water meters (water meter with pulse output 10 or 100l / pulse
  • Impulse output Energy-Volume
  • LCD Display:
  • Read data in 2 loops, control by button on front of calorimeter. Press the button for more than 2 seconds to move to the second loop
