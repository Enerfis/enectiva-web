Domestic water meter Flodis FL25

Single-flow dry-running domestic water meter for SV, cl. Precision C, is suitable for cross-section reduction and as a replacement for obsolete water meters, has high accuracy over a wide range of flows, low starting flow, very low pressure loss. It has long-lasting metrological parameters. It is permanently loadable on Qmax and highly overloaded.
