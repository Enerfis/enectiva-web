CF-UltraMaXX MK



CF-UltraMaXX MK

CF Ultrasonic Heat Meter in Capsule Design is compatible with the Integral MK MaXX EAT Assembly Kit. It has class 2 of accuracy according to ČSN EN1434, environment class C. It is characterized by advanced features and diagnostics, easy installation, minimal maintenance and long service life. Applications: Heating, cooling, combined heating and cooling.


Technical Specifications:
  • Temperature range 0-90°C / 0 – 150°C
  • Temperature difference: 3-90 / 3 – 150 K
  • Power supply: 6 + 1 years
  • Degree of protection: IP64
  • Energy units: kWh-G

  • Type: PT500
  • Design: DS, reservoir
  • Cable length: 2 x 1.2 m
  • External communication:
  • Optional communication cards:
  • -M BUS

  • -M BUS/4WM
  • -radio 433 MHz

Tech. Parameters-flowmeter:
  • Operating temperature: 90 ° C
  • Nominal pressure: PN 16
  • IP 67
  • Connecting cable: 0.5m


LCD Display
  • Reads data in 3 loops
  • Loop 1: billing and balance data
  • Loop 2: automatic monthly readings
  • Loop 3: Operating and service data
