Residential water meter T4 Unimag Cyble-25

Residential water meter T4 Unimag Cyble-25 is a single-flow, dry-water meter for SV and TUV. It has a standard pre-equipped counter for installing a CYBLE inductive type transmitter, the CYBLE transmitter allows remote reading of the meter or its connection to control systems. It is characterized by long-term stability of metrological parameters, high reliability and long service life.

Specifications
  • Installation in horizontal and vertical position
  • DN20
  • Qn=2,5
  • L=130
  • Thread = 4/4 ''
  • Accuracy +/- 5% Qmin dm3 / h 30
  • Temperature range SV 30 ° C; DHW 90 ° C
  • Horizontal installation - accuracy class B
  • Vertical and other installations - accuracy class A
  • Hermetically sealed counter
  • Rotary counter by 360 °
  • Eight-walled numeric pointer, five for m3, three for smaller values
  • Hand indicator for smaller values
  • Transmitter versions:
  • -Cyble NF - 1 pulse output
  • -Cyble NF + UF - 2 pulse outputs
  • -Cyble MBUS - data transfer in the M-BUS protocol
  • -Cyble RF - radio transmission of data in the 433 MHz band, Radian protocol
