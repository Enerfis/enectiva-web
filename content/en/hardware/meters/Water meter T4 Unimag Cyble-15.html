Residential water meter T4 Unimag Cyble-15

Single-flow, dry-running water meter without T4 Unimag Cyble-15 bypass control for SV and DHW is designed for horizontal and vertical mounting. The CYBLE transmitter is pre-fitted with a CYBLE induction transmitter counter, the CYBLE transmitter allows remote reading of the meter or its connection to the control systems. It is characterized by long-term stability of metrological parameters. Another advantage is high reliability and long service life.
