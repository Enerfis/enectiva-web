ED310 – 3 phase electricity meter on DIN rail

The ED310 is a three-phase four-energy energy meter class A or B active energy meter designed for direct (ED310) and indirect (ED310.I) connections. It converts the analogue signal from the current and voltage sensor to digital, performs calculations, controls the display, senses tariff inputs, communicates over the opto interface, generates IR and S pulses, and stores the selected values and data, and adapts the meter's properties to the requirements and needs of the customer. The measuring system allows measurement even in the presence of DC and harmonic components in the measured circuit throughout the measuring range of the meter. The calibration of the measuring system is done programmatically.


    Features:
  • Montage on DIN rail
  • electricity for retail customers
  • Measuring power in both directions
  • Electronic (with display)
  • 1-4 tariffs)
  • Tariff control - external
  • Direct and indirect measurements
  • Interface S, RS485, M-Bus
    The meter measures, stores and displays quantities:
  • For each of the 4 fares, consumption and delivery
  • For each consumption and supply register the counting time for this register
  • Total registers for total subscription time and total delivery time
  • Maximum current and maximum power
  • Operating time, number of network outages, time to reset maximum current and power
  • Instantaneous effective tension
  • Instantaneous efficient stream
  • Immediate active power
