MGDIZ - DIZ Industrial Electricity Meter

MGDIZ meters are two-digit digital meters for active energy measurement. They are designed for industrial applications and for billing purposes. They are compact in design, widths of only 6 TE, and also for measurement of supply and delivery. M-Bus or RS485 communication. They have an 8-digit LCD display, adjustable converter, and a battery. Certificate ČMI - TCM 221 / 05-4240.
