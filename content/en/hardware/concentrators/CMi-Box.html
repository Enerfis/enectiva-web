CMi-Box

CMi-Box is a flexble solution for collecting meter values. Can be customized to each costumer’s need. Compatible with an meter that follows the M-Bus standar protocol.



Technical Data:
  • Dimensions (wxhxd): 141x157x44mm
  • Weight: 460g
  • Mounting: wall-mounted with bracket
  • Power supply: 230V, 3m cable included; 24V, PCB push connector.
  • Power consumption: <2.5W
  • nstallation category: CAT2

Operating values:
  • Temperature: -30ºC to +55ºC
  • Humidity: 80 % RH at temperatures up to 31 °C, decreasing linearly to 50 % RH at 40 °C

Interfaces:
  • M-Bus Master (EN 13757), max 7 (software limit)
  • Wireless M-Bus Receiver (EN 13757-4), max 800 (software limit)
