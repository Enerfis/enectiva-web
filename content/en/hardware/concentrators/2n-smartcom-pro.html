2N® SmartCom PRO

The 2N SmartCom PRO is a complete M2M (Machine-to-Machine) solution developed for the wireless centralized management of the endpoint devices and remote systems. It is the most used data concentrator in the Enectiva system. The firmware of this version is adjusted so that the communication between the meters and the Enectiva server is smooth and does not require setting up of the individual interfaces. If you order the concentrator from our company, you simply connect it to the power grid and it starts to communicate with the Enectiva server immediately. After connecting the meters, you are ready to use all the services of the Enectiva energy management. All types of meters using various interfaces may be connected to the 2N SmartCom PRO concentrator. Supported interfaces are Wireless M-bus, ZigBee, RS485, RS232, M-bus and S0.



GSM/GPRS
  • Frequency bands: GSM 850/ 900/ 1800/ 1900 MHz
  • GSM module: Sierra Wireless WISMO228
  • SIM card: 3V/1.8V
  • GPRS Class 10
  • Antenna: 50 Ω SMA connector

Interfaces
  • RS232
  • RS485, M-Bus or second RS232

2x built-in relays:
  • Max. carrying current: 2A
  • Max. switching voltage: 250 VAC / 220VDC
  • Max. switching power: 62.5VA / 30W

2x configurable inputs:
  • Analog 0-20mA
  • Analog 0-10V
  • Digital with adjustable level
  • S0 pulse input

Optional Interfaces
  • Ethernet: 100Mb/s
  • Wireless M-Bus: 868MHz, 169MHz, 433MHz
  • IEEE 802.15.4: ZigBee, Zigbee Smart Energy profile, 6LoWPAN
  • Other wireless: KNX RF, RC 232, TinyMesh

Power
  • Power supply: 5-50V DC
  • Power consumption: Up to 7W
  • Backup: External 6/12V Pb battery with automatic charging

Physical & Environmental
  • Dimensions: 100x105x30 mm
  • Operating temperature range from -40°C to + 85 °C
  • Operating humidity from 0 % to 80%
