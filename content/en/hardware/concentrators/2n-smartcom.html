2N® SmartCom

2N SmartCom is an intelligent device that allows you to communicate and monitor your assets.  Just connect it to an asset, set the IP address for data delivery, and you can begin. It is a simpler version of a dependable, a widely used concentrator SmartCom PRO manufactured by the 2N Telekomunikace company. The firmware has been adjusted for the purpose of the Enectiva ecosystem and it enables you to connect the concentrator to the Enectiva server in a plug-and-play fashion. All you need to do is to connect the meters. The communication with the server can be realized through the SIM cards or through Ethernet. Supported interfaces are RS232, M-bus,RS485,S0.



GSM/GPRS
  • Frequency bands: GSM 850/ 900/ 1800/ 1900 MHz
  • GSM module: Sierra Wireless WISMO228
  • SIM card: 3V/1.8V
  • GPRS Class 10
  • Antenna: 50 Ω SMA connector

2x built-in relays:
  • Max. carrying current: 2A
  • Max. switching voltage: 250 VAC / 220VDC
  • Max. switching power: 62.5VA / 30W

Power supply
  • Power consumption: Max 3W
  • Mains supply: Adapter 100-240V/50-60Hz to 12V/0.5A

Interface
  • RS232 + RS485
  • RS232 + Mbus

2x Configurable inputs
  • Analog 0-20mA
  • Analog 0-10V
  • Digital input: Logical Level Monitoring

Physical & Environmental
  • Dimensions: 69 x 100 x 24 mm
  • Operating temperature range from -40°C to 85°C
  • Operating humidity from 0 % to 80%
