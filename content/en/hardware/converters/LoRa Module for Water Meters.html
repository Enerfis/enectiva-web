LoRa Module for Water Meters

LoRa connectivity module is compatible with Sensus water meters. Availiable also for other water meter types by request.

  • The unit contains a read head and a transmitter unit
  • Compatible with Sensus water meters. Availiable also for other water meter types by request.
  • Antenna 2 dBm and battery 3,6V 2400 m Ah included in the package.
