M-bus Splitter

M-Bus Splitter allows to connect up to 4 M-Bus meters to 2 M-Bus masters. With this device it is possible to connect the same meter to 2 independent networks and read data simultaneously.

  • Works with all devices accroding to EN1434
  • LED status indicators
  • Baud rate: 300/2400
  • Power: M-Bus or 24V
  • Mounting: DIN rail or wall
  • Operating Temperature: 0°C / 55°C
