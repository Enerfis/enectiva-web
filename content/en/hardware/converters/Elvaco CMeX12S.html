Elvaco CMeX12S

Permite expandir su serie Cme hasta 128 M-Bus esclavos. Se puede conectar con cualquier sistema M-Bus.



Technical Data:
  • Dimensions (wxhxd): 108x90x65 mm (7 DIN modules)
  • Weight: 220g
  • Mounting DIN mounteed
  • Power consumption: max 25W
  • Power consuption (nom) 1.5 mA x M-Bus slaves + 1W
  • Nominal voltage: 100-240 VAC

Operating values:
  • Temperature -30ºC to +55ºC
  • Humidity max 80% RH temperatures up to 31ºC decreasing linearly to 50% RH at 40ºC

Connections:
  • M-Bus Standar EN 13757
  • Max. 128 M-Bus connected
  • RS232 to M-Bus
  • Max cable lenght 5000m
  • Compatibility: All M-Bus meters, all ABB meters with IR interface, CmeX Series products.
