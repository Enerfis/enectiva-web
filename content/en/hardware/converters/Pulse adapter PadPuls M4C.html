Pulse adapter PadPuls M4C

PadPuls M4C is an impulse adapter that connects up to four conventional pulse outputs to the M-Bus system, is designed for DIN or wall mounting DIN EN 50022


Specifications
  • Size: 58 x 53 x 91 (LxWxH)
  • Mounting: DIN EN 50022 rail
  • Operating temperature: 0-55ÂºC
  • Operating humidity: 10% - 70%
  • 4 separate pulse inputs
  • Detects up to 18 Pulses per second for each output
  • Adjustable pulse value
  • Freely adjustable units (Wh, kWh, MWh, kJ, m3 ...)
  • Counter length: 8 decimal places
  • Due-date function with integrated real time
  • M-Bus protocol according to EN13757-2 and 3
  • Fully configurable with M-Bus or Optical Interface, including rewriting protection
  • Baud rate: 300/2400/9600
  • Fully functional in case of M-Bus failure thanks to integrated battery.
  • Counter and pulse values are freely adjustable
  • Tariff mode: 2 pulse inputs and two tariff switching inputs or 230 VAC input
  • Pulse input state is indicated
