CYBLE MBus - Itron communication module for remote reading with M-Bus Protocol.

CYBLE MBus works on the same rotating pointer scanning principle as the CYBLE pulse transmitter. This guarantees absolute data match with the water meter counter. It can be installed on all water meters pre-fitted for CYBLE installation. The high degree of protection ensures reliable operation even in case of long-term immersion.



Basic Transmitted Data:
  • Date and Time
  • Water meter reading status
  • Customer ID (10 digit alpha-numerical)
  • Counter status at the end of the previous month
  • Backflow detection
  • Peak flow management (memorization over the last 13 months).
  • Primary and secondary address
  • Fault messages
  • Leakage Detection
  • Low battery voltage

Extended data transfer
  • Deduction to a fixed date
  • Recording of leakage (13 months memory)
  • Backflow recording (13 month memory)

Data security:
  • Secured by access passwords
  • Efforts to influence the reading are signaled when reading
  • There is no data loss in the MBus line fault

Using the system
  • Billing metrics for sites
  • Billing deductions for buildings with high concentration of gauges
  • Network tracking, max flow, leakage, backflow
  • Datalogging - aut. Reading at regular intervals provides flow information on all measured locations connected to the system
