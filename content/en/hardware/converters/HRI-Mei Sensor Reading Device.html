HRI-MEI is a communications member of MeiStream, MeiStream Plus and Mei Twin Water Meters

HRI-MEI is a data interface for pulse output of industrial water meters. Due to the IP68 protection, this member works even when flooded with water. It can be retrofitted without damaging the water meter seal.



Use:
  • Industrial monitoring and control applications, SCADA systems
  • Flow indication via frequency inverters such as FM-1D / K or FM-2D / K
  • Local readings for billing applications
  • Remote readings via cable networks with M-Bus, radio modem, or GSM network

Advantages:
  • Compatible with commercial and industrial water meters such as MeiStream and MeinTwin
  • Clip fastening without interruption of the water meter seal
  • Detection of water flow direction
  • Supports other applications requiring mechanical switches or optical pulse outputs

Properties:
  • Inductive scanning of the meter pointer
  • Self-diagnosis for reading detection
  • Battery life up to 12 years
  • Robust design and sealed case (IP68
  • 3 m cable
Data interfaces:
  • M-Bus to minibus (automaticky)
  • Automatic baud rate detection (300/2400 Bd) and interface type
  • Data protocol according to IEC870-5 / EN13757-3

Uploaded data:
  • Gauge status
  • Monthly status for a particular day
  • Min./Max. Water flow with date / time
  • Status for the specified date of the year and last year
  • Network tracking, max flow, leakage, backflow
  • Unauthorized interference
