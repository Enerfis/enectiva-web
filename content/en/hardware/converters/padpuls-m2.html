PadPuls M2

To monitor your water or electricity consumption using Enectiva, we need to centrally read the data by M-Bus. That can be achieved by the 2-channel pulse adaptor PadPuls M2 that allows the use of up to 2 meters with pulse output as M-Bus slaves. The transmission rate is from 300 to 2400 bps and there is a safe detection of up to 15 pulses per second at each input.

  • Two separated pulse inputs
  • Safe detection of up to 15 pulses per second at each input
  • Pulse debouncing
  • Pulse value free adjustable
  • Unit free selectable (e.g. Wh, kWh, MWh, kJ, m3, l etc.)
  • Counter length: 8 decimal digits
  • M-Bus protocol according to EN13757-2 and -3
  • Fully parameterized by M-Bus, including overwrite protection
  • Transmission rate: 300 and 2400 baud with auto baud detection
  • Supplied by M-Bus, or integrated battery at an M-Bus failure, needs no power supply unit.
  • Dimension: W x H x D: 80 x 80 x 52 mm
  • Two battery options available
  • Temperature range: 0°C to 55°C
