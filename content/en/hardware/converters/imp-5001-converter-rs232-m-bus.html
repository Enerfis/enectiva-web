IMP 5001 Converter RS232/M-Bus

In Enectiva we use the device IMP 5001 to convert RS232 to M-Bus interface. It can be used by all devices that use M-Bus interface. IMP 5001 can be used for the transmission rate from 300 to 9600bps with transfer from master to slave or from slave to master being executed by bus voltage or slave current.

  • RS232C to M-Bus interface converter
  • Complies with the EN 1434.3 standard
  • Hardware design with switched power source and low energy dissipation
  • Transmission rate from 300 bps to 9600 bps.
