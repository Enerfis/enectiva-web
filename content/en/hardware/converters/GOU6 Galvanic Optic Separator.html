GOU 6 Galvanic Optical Separator GOU6

GOU 6 is six channel unidirectional galvanic separator for electricity meter output. It used to separate an output from industrial and utility electricity meters.


Technical Specifications:
  • Size: 90 x 71 x 55 mm (LxWxH)
  • Mounting: DIN rail 35mm
  • Environment: mormant according to ČSN 33 2000-3
  • Power: 80-250 VAC 50 HZ, red LED indicator
  • AC Input
  • Number 1 marked IN A
  • Wiring: against terminal N (L) (neutral)
  • Voltage 80-250VAC(connected)
  • Input resistance: 110 kΩ
  • Pulse length: min. 100ms
  • Cable: max. 3 m
  • Warning, AC IN A do not use DC IN1 at the same time!
  • DC Inputs:
  • Number of inputs 6 marked IN1 to IN6 with common COM field
  • Voltage / current 5 VDC / max 5 mA - against COM (powered from internal source GOU 6)
  • Pulse length min. 100 ms for IN1, min. 10 ms for IN2 to IN6
  • Supply lead max. 3 m
  • Warning, Do not use DC IN 1 at the same time as AC IN A!
  • DC outputs:
  • The number of outputs 6 marked OUT1 to OUT6
  • Indication 1x yellow LED for OUT1
  • 5x green LED for OUT2 to OUT6
  • Voltage 5 to 30 VDC, current max. 30 mA
  • Pulse length for OUT1 copies / inverts input IN1 or IN A (DIP 3)
  • For OUT2 to OUT6 selectable 50, 100, 200, 720 ms (DIP 1 and 2)
  • Dividing ratio for OUT1 does not divide
  • For OUT2 to OUT6: 1,: 4,: 8,: 16 input impulses (DIP 5, 6 and DIP 7, 8)
  • Galvanic separator type GOU 6 always use to separate only one meter!
