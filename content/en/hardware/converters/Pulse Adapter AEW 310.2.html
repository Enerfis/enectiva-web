Pulse Adapter AEW 310.2

The Pulse M-Bus Adapter AEW310.2 is part of the M-Bus system. It is used for pulse output consumption meters where it is required to collect the consumption data into the M-Bus system. Such meters may be water meters on the TV, SV water meters, gas meters, electricity meters, etc. The pulse adapter must be set before the bus connection, parameter setting is performed via the M-Bus interface.


Function:
  • Consumption data reading from pulse output
  • Type of pulse source monitoring( Reed, NAMUR, Open Collector)
  • Data storage, including consumption and date dni
  • Remote data transfer by M-Bus request

Possible types of connected meters:
  • Water meters with impulse output
  • Heat meters with impulse output
  • Gas meters with impulse output
  • Steam meter with impulse output
  • Power meters with S0 interface
