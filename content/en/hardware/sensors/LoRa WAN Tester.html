LoRa Tester

To plan sensor layout it is necessary to assess quality of the network in the specific location with LoR WAN tester.

Tester properties

  • OLED Display - Displays RSSI and SNR
  • Control via qtouch touch
  • Option to change parameters in field, SF, ADR, autosleep ...
  • Switching to low power mode and sensor simulation - periodic sending of payloads
  • Built-in battery, 30 hours of continuous operation
  • Configuration and charging via USB
