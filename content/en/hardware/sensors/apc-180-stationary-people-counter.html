APC-180 Stationary People Counter

To get an exact idea about how many people come and go into your building or office, we are installing the precise people counter from Hella. It works with a great accuracy under variable lightning or shadows thanks to an image processing captured with 3D camera technology.

  • Operating nominal voltage, Power-over-Ethernet (IEEE 802.3af, PD class 0): 36…57 V DC
  • Power consumption (PD class 0): 4 W (additional 2.5 W using USB port)
  • Operating temperature from -25 °C to +70 °C
  • Installation height from 2.50 m to 6.00 m
  • Monitored area: 8 m x 8 m
  • Illuminance (at least): 30 Lux
  • Ethernet: 100BaseT, PoE, RJ45
  • Logical protocol: SOAP/XML, Message API, IBIS, SNMP and, data recording upload via FTP/SFTP
