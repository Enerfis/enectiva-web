Ambient temperature and humidity transmitter (169 MHz)

Ambient temperature and humidity transmitter is another sensor used by Enectiva. It operates on a frequency of 169 MHz and it communicates wirelessly with M-Bus. The French product is deployed when we are in need of reliable data of the temperature and humidity in order to optimize your indoor environment.
