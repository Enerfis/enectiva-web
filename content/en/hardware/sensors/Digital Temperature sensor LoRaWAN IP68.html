Digital Temperature sensor LoRaWAN IP68

Digital temperature sensorprimarily for food industry. Sensor is situated in the food hose, allowing to measure temperature in places where cleanliness is of the essence.

  • Temperature Range: from – 55 °C to +155 °C
  • Measurement resolution 0.1 ° C
  • Measuring accuracy +/- 0.5 ° C
  • Battery power
  • Very low power consumption in sleep mode (only 4uA
  • Long battery life (about 5 years)
  • Class A sensor
  • Protection IP 68
  • Remotely adjustable measurement period
