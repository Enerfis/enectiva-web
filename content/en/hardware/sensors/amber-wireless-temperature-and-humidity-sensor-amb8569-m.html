Amber Wireless temperature and humidity sensor AMB8569-M

In Enectiva we are in need of reliable data of the temperature and humidity in order to optimize your heating systems to perform as efficient as possible. Therefore, the AMB8569-M is a perfect tool for delivering those data to our database. It sends the information every 15 minutes via wireless M-Bus so that it can be used for visualization and post processing instantly. It operates on a frequency of 868 MHz.

  • Measurement of temperature and humidity
  • Reading interval of 5 or 15 minutes
  • Connects directly to M-Bus devices
  • Wireless M-Bus (EN13757-4) standard
  • Wireless M-Bus modes: S and T selectable
  • Supports data encryption: AES128 (mode 5) or CMAC (mode 7)
  • Sensor temperature range from -40°C to 85°C
  • Humidity sensor resolution: +/- 2% RH
  • Power supply: replaceable 3.6V LiIthium battery
  • Fixed frequencies 868.3 / 868.95 MHz
  • RF data rate 16.384 / 66.6 kbps
  • Modulation type: 2-FSK
  • Dimensions: 65 x 50 x 30 mm
  • Weight: 75g
  • IP rating: IP20
